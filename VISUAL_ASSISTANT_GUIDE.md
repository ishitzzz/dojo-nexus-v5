# 📸 Visual Assistant - Learning Companion

## What It Does
The **Visual Assistant** allows you to upload a screenshot of any learning material (video frame, code snippet, diagram) and get an instant AI explanation.

---

## How to Use

### Step 1: Open a Chapter
1. Navigate to any module in your roadmap
2. Click on a chapter to open the Workspace

### Step 2: Capture Your Screen
1. Pause the video at the exact moment you want explained
2. Take a screenshot using your system tools:
   - **Windows**: Press `Win + Shift + S`
   - **Mac**: Press `Cmd + Shift + 4`
   - **Linux**: Use your preferred screenshot tool

### Step 3: Upload to Chat
1. Click the **"🤖 AI Chat"** tab in the left panel
2. Click the **"📸 Upload Screenshot"** button
3. Select your screenshot file
4. The AI will analyze it immediately

### Step 4: Get AI Explanation
1. Groq Vision AI analyzes the image
2. You get a detailed explanation based on:
   - The visual content (code, diagrams, UI)
   - The video transcript (context)
   - The current chapter/module topic

---

## Technical Details

### Why Manual Upload?
Browser security policies (CORS) prevent websites from programmatically capturing exact frames from YouTube videos. To ensure you get **100% accurate, high-quality analysis** of exactly what you see, a manual screenshot is the best solution.

### Privacy & Security
- Screenshots are processed only for the analysis
- No images are permanently stored on our servers
- AI analysis is performed via secure API calls

---

## Tips for Best Results
- **Capture clear images**: Ensure text and code are readable
- **Include context**: If possible, capture surrounding UI or headers
- **Ask follow-up questions**: After the initial analysis, you can chat with the AI about the image
